# 🚀 RegFlow AI

> **An Agentic Compliance Operating System for SEBI-Regulated Intermediaries**

Transforming Regulatory Text into Operational Action using Agentic AI.

---

## 📖 Overview

RegFlow AI is an AI-powered compliance platform designed to bridge the gap between regulatory documents and operational compliance.

Instead of simply answering questions about SEBI circulars, RegFlow AI automatically converts regulatory text into structured compliance workflows by extracting obligations, assigning responsibilities, generating tasks, validating evidence, and producing audit-ready reports.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Financial intermediaries regulated by SEBI must continuously comply with evolving circulars, notifications, and master regulations.

Current compliance processes involve:

- Manual document review
- Legal interpretation
- Spreadsheet tracking
- Manual task assignment
- Scattered evidence
- Time-consuming audits

These processes are slow, expensive, and prone to human error.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

RegFlow AI introduces an **Agentic Compliance Operating System** that automates the entire compliance lifecycle.

The platform:

- Reads SEBI Circulars
- Extracts Regulatory Obligations
- Maps Responsibilities
- Generates Compliance Tasks
- Tracks Deadlines
- Validates Evidence
- Produces Audit Reports

All while keeping humans in control through Human-in-the-Loop validation.
